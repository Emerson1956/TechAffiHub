What Is a Digital Business?

A digital business is any business that uses the internet and digital tools to provide products or services. Unlike traditional brick-and-mortar setups, digital businesses operate entirely online â€” whether it's selling eBooks, coaching clients via Zoom, running a SaaS platform, or affiliate marketing.

Step 1: Identify a Profitable Digital Business Idea

Choose a niche where market demand, your skills, and passion intersect. Popular ideas in 2025 include:

  • Online coaching or consulting
  • Subscription-based SaaS products
  • Affiliate marketing or product reviews
  • Creating and selling digital courses
  • Print-on-demand eCommerce
  • YouTube, TikTok, or content creation

Use tools like Google Trends, Exploding Topics, or Ubersuggest to explore niche popularity.

Step 2: Validate Your Idea Before Launching

Before building, test your idea by:

  • Creating a landing page with tools like Systeme.io or Leadpages
  • Running pre-launch surveys using Google Forms
  • Starting a waitlist or beta group
  • Posting MVP offers on social media or Reddit

Validation saves time and resources. If you receive sign-ups or real interest, your idea is worth developing.

Step 4: Build & Launch Your First Offer

Create your Minimum Viable Product (MVP). This could be:

  • An eBook or course
  • Digital service package
  • Freemium software trial

Use Gumroad or Payhip to deliver digital products securely. Add testimonials, benefits, and bonuses to build urgency.

Step 5: Market Your Business Online
